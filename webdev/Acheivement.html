Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam.

The Government of India honoured Dr Kalam with Padma Bhushan in 1981, and after nine years, he was honoured with Padma Vibhushan as well. In 1997, Dr Kalam was honoured with the country's highest civilian honour Bharat Ratna. The same year, he also received Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration
